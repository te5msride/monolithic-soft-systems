Monolithic Soft Systems: Co-design tools, materials, and fabrication strategies for next-generation robotics

Proposed Workshop for IEEE RoboSoft 2026

About

This workshop brings together researchers developing integrated approaches for the co-design, fabrication, and modeling of monolithic soft robotic systems. Our goal is to identify emerging methods that merge material, design, and control domains to push the boundaries of embodied intelligence.
