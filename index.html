Overview

This workshop unites researchers advancing integrated approaches for co-design, fabrication, modeling, and control of monolithic soft robotic systems. We aim to surface emerging methods that merge material, design, and control domains to push the boundaries of embodied intelligence.

  • Co-design frameworks, digital twins, and multiphysics simulation

  • Applications, benchmarks, and reproducible workflows
